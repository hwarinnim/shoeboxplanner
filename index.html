Shoebox Planner

Plan your space, your way.

This app helps you map out your room and rearrange furniture to find the perfect fit.

"Free Mode" let's you place items anywhere, even overlapping. Perfect for visualizing layered spaces like rugs under beds or shelves above desks.

"Tidy Mode" keeps items from overlapping so you can check what actually fits side by side.
