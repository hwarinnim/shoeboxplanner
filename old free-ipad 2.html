How to Use Shoebox Planner:

1. Set your room size.
Enter your room's width and height to create the space. Use the unit toggle to choose between cm or in.

2. Add your furniture.
Use squares/rectangles for most items and circles/ovals for round ones.

3. Adjust if needed.
Rotate items to fit how they really sit in your space. Adjust font size to fit the size of your item.

4. Arrange your layout.
Drag items into place and rearrange item layers. Hide or delete items as needed.

5. Undo / Redo / Reset / Clear.
Fix mistakes or start fresh anytime.

6. Save your layout.
Keep a copy of your plan for later or export it as an image file.
